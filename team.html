About Us

Who We Are

In the vast expanse of creativity, six innovators collided at the crossroads of passion and challenge. We are AstroNerds, a group of tech enthusiasts who ventured into a 48-hour hackathon, fueled by coffee, ambition, and the dream to build something extraordinary. What started as a spark of ideas quickly turned into a universe of possibilities. Together, we brainstormed, coded, and designed our way through the hackathon, turning complex problems into simple, impactful solutions. We thrive on pushing boundaries, breaking limits, and thinking in dimensions beyond the ordinary. Our mission? To bring technology, creativity, and community closer together. Through collaboration, we believe that we can shape the future and inspire others to join us in this cosmic journey of innovation. This is just the beginning—we’re here to leave our mark in the stars!.
